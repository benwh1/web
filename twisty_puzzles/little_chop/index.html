UF UB UL UR UL UB

Rotate U face clockwise and preserve permutation of F face (11)
UL UB UL UR UL FL FR FL UB UL UR

Swap UL/FL, preserve permutation of other U/F faces (15) (this is the shortest 2-cycle of a U piece with an F piece, ignoring permutation of the other 4 faces)
UF FL UR UF UR FR UF UB UF FR UR UF UR FL UF
